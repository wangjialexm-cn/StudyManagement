# Day5 英语 Answer

## Class Practice

1. am。解析：主语 I 固定搭配 am。
2. is。解析：She 是第三人称单数，进行时用 is doing。
3. are。解析：They 是复数，进行时用 are doing。
4. is。解析：The number 是单数，所以用 is increasing。
5. are。解析：The students 是复数，所以用 are listening。
6. The number is increasing. 解析：现在进行时需要 be + doing，increase 要变为 increasing。
7. The price is decreasing. 解析：The price 是单数，用 is；decrease 变为 decreasing。
8. The students are learning. 解析：students 是复数，be 动词应为 are。
9. Success depends on hard work. 解析：Success 是单数概念，一般现在时动词用三单 depends。
10. She is doing her homework. 解析：“正在”对应 is doing。
11. The students are learning English. 解析：复数主语 students 对应 are。
12. Success depends on daily practice. 解析：depend on 用于一般现在时，单数主语加 s。
13. The price is decreasing. 解析：“正在下降”用 is decreasing。

## Homework

1. am。解析：I 与 am 搭配。

3. are。解析：The students 是复数，进行时用 are doing。
4. increasing。解析：be 动词后接 doing。
5. decreasing。解析：be 动词后接 doing。
6. She is writing. 解析：She 不能搭配 are。
7. The students are learning. 解析：复数主语要用 are。
8. Success depends on practice. 解析：Success 是单数概念，depend 要加 s。
9. The number is increasing. 解析：进行时结构为 is increasing。
10. I am learning English. 解析：I 对应 am，正在学习用 learning。
11. She is discovering a new method. 解析：discover 的现在分词为 discovering。
12. Success depends on hard work. 解析：三单句，depends on。
13. The students are improving their writing. 解析：students 是复数，用 are improving。
14. 修改后：The students are learning English now. Their vocabulary is increasing. The teacher is helping them. Good progress depends on daily practice. 解析：students 复数用 are；vocabulary 和 teacher 为单数用 is；progress 是单数概念，depend 加 s。
15. 80词写作参考：I am practicing English every day. My vocabulary is increasing because I read and write more. My progress depends on daily practice, not only on memory. I am learning how to use be plus doing correctly. I especially want to improve translation and short writing. When I write a sentence, I check the subject first, then the tense, and finally the verb form.
16. 80词写作参考：Learning depends on daily practice. I am practicing English every day, and my vocabulary is increasing slowly. Good progress does not depend on luck. It depends on small habits, especially reading, translating, and writing sentences. I discovered that grammar becomes clearer when I use it in real sentences. Now I am trying to check the subject first and then choose the correct verb form. This method helps me write more carefully.
