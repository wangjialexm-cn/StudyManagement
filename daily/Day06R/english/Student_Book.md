## Reading｜Vocabulary Depth

Many students remember the Chinese meanings of new words, but they still make mistakes when they write. The reason is simple: a word must enter the sentence system. A learner should know its part of speech, common collocations, and verb forms. For example, increase and decrease can be verbs. If students discover this, their output will become clearer. Progress depends on daily practice, especially sentence writing. When active output increases, grammar mistakes often decrease.

## Reading Tasks

1. What problem do many students have?
2. What should a learner know about a word?
3. Why are increase and decrease important examples?
4. Translate: Progress depends on daily practice.
5. Find one sentence with especially.

## Writing Method｜80词输出

写作题：Vocabulary Depth Helps Me Learn English

要求：

- 使用 5 个雷达词；
- 至少 6 句；
- 句子准确优先；
- 不追求复杂从句。

## 方法总结

英语输出四步：

1. 先判断词性；
2. 再找搭配；
3. 决定动词形式；
4. 放入完整句子。

## 最容易错的地方

- 知道中文但不知道词性。
- 认识 depend on，但忘记 depends on。
- especially 当成动词使用。
- 阅读中只逐词翻译，不抓句子主干。

## 本节一句话总结

英语词汇要从“会认”升级到“会在阅读和写作中主动使用”。
